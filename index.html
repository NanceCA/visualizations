Collective Academy - Sample Student Visualization

The following visualizations are based on mock-ups received by Collective Academy staff on 01/09/2020. The first chart below is intended to be nestled in the student view on the Eva platform. The second is intended to be nestled under the administrative/manager platform. The visualizations are built using the High Charts Javascript framework.

Please provide feedback on the following items:

Item Feedback Questions
Look and feel Are the graphs displaying the ideal color scheme?

Usability For the aggregate graph, is a column chart the most appropriate given the volume of the stuents in each cohort?
Competency types How many competencies are there? Are they fixed (unchanging)?
